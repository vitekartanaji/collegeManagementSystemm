Dear Students

The SangolaTaluka Uchch ShikshanMandal has been established by three visionary persons late SambhajiraoShende, late

Bapusaheb Zapake and late Bajrang Lonkhande and others in the year 1970 with

the motto ‘Dnyandeep Lau Jagi’.


This college is established in the year 1978 with view to provide Higher Education facilities to students

from Sangola and around draught prone area and to uplift these families socially and financially. The

college which started with only 50 students has now student-strength more than 2500. College

management always supports the College administration without interfering daily routine.


The basic infrastructure is adequate for all the faculties. Management tries to provide all the necessary

facilities for ICT based teaching, learning and evaluation. Identifying the need to enhance soft skills, job skills

and employability of students, in the year 2000, Computer Science Department was started. It has

now became unique Department in the university with B.Sc(ECS),BCA and M.Sc(CS) programs, having highest students strength.


I am proud to state that students get placed in renowned Industries like TCS, SYNTEL, WIPRO, Tech-Mahindra,

Quick Heal, ICICI Bank, I-Gate Patani, Capgemini ,etc.


and college administration provide facilities necessary for holistic development of students.
