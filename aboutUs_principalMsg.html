Dear Students

It is my privilege to welcome you all in this college affiliated to Punyshlok Ahilyadevi Holakar Solapur University, Solapur.

This college is established in the year 1978 with motto “Dnyandeep Lau Jagi” to make available facility of Higher Education

to students from this area and around. College is located on Sangola- Jat road in a beautiful campus admeasuring 7.75 acres.

Four U.G. programmes (B.A., B.Sc., B.Com & B.C.A.) and two P.G. programmes [M.A.(Marathi , Hindi)., M.Sc. (Comp. Science .,

Organic Chemistry) are run in the college. Three research centers (Marathi, Economics and geography) are recognized by the University.


Experienced and dedicated faculty work hard and sincerely for holistic development of the students. Administrative staff extend their

prompt and humble services to students and other stake holders.


During the COVD -19 pandemic, college has provided all the necessary facilities for online teaching, learning and evaluation. Counseling

sessions were organized for students on awareness regarding COVID – 19. Counseling of students to encourage them for vaccination is held

and vaccination programme is also organized.


College provides all the necessary facilities viz. well-equipped science laboratories, computer laboratories, English laboratory, automated

library with spacious reading hall, sports complex with play-ground having a large running track of 4X 800 size, gents and ladies hostels,

career counseling and placement cell etc. To enhance employability of the students, competitive Examinations guidance center provides guidance

to aspiring students. Skill based job oriented short term courses are run to enhance job potential of students.


NCC,NSS and cultural units in the college play vital role in development of personality and leadership attitude among students. Also, these units

build competence among students. Many NCC cadets have been recruited in defense force.


Computer Science Department of the college is a renowned department in the University and MNCs. Our more than 2000 students are working in many MNCs

like INFOSYS, WIPRO, CAPE-GEMINI, TCS, Tech-Mahindra etc. on prestigious positions .
